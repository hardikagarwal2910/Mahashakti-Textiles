SUSTAINABILITY

40% renewable today. 100% on the roadmap.

After yarn, electricity is a weaving mill's biggest input. Around 40% of ours already comes from renewable sources — and the roadmap to 100% is set. What follows is what we actually do, in numbers.

Powering 200 looms responsibly.

01 — ENERGY

Air-jet weaving is energy-intensive by nature — compressed air does the work of the shuttle. That makes the source of our electricity a first-order decision, not a CSR footnote. Today around 40% of the mill's electricity comes from renewable sources, and successive phases of renewable capacity are planned to take that to 100%.
